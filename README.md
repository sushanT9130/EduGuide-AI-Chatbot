# EduGuide AI – Smart Educational Assistant

## 📖 Overview

EduGuide AI is an AI-powered educational chatbot developed to support **United Nations Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 4: Quality Education**. The chatbot provides students with instant academic assistance, career guidance, scholarship information, exam preparation support, and study planning through an interactive conversational interface.

The objective of this project is to make educational guidance accessible to all students, especially those who may not have continuous access to teachers, mentors, or career counselors.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many students, particularly in rural and underserved communities, face challenges in accessing quality educational support, career counseling, scholarship information, and exam preparation resources. This lack of guidance can limit academic success and future opportunities.

---

## 💡 Solution

EduGuide AI addresses this problem by offering:

- 📚 Academic doubt solving
- 🎓 Scholarship and educational opportunity guidance
- 🚀 Career counseling and skill development recommendations
- 📝 Exam preparation assistance
- 📅 Personalized study planning
- 🌐 Real-time educational information through AI-powered conversations

The chatbot is available 24/7 and provides students with immediate access to educational support.

---

## 🌍 Sustainable Development Goal (SDG)

### SDG 4 – Quality Education

**Target:** Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

EduGuide AI contributes to this goal by making educational resources and guidance easily accessible through artificial intelligence.
